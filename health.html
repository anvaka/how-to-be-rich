How to Be Rich in Health

Nourish Your Body

Feed yourself with intention and awareness. Quality nutrition is the foundation of vibrant health.

Try These
  • Eat a rainbow of whole foods daily
  • Practice mindful eating without distractions
  • Stay hydrated with clean water throughout the day
Move with Joy

Find physical activities that bring you pleasure. Sustainable exercise comes from enjoying movement.

Move More
  • Try different forms of movement until you find your joy
  • Make movement social with friends or groups
  • Take movement snacks throughout the day
Master Rest and Recovery

Prioritize quality sleep and downtime. Rest is when the body repairs and regenerates.

Rest Well
  • Create a calm bedtime routine
  • Practice strategic rest between activities
  • Listen to your body's need for recovery
Cultivate Mental Fitness

Exercise your mind like a muscle. Mental health is integral to overall well-being.

Mind Work
  • Practice daily meditation or mindfulness
  • Challenge yourself with new learning
  • Seek professional support when needed
Build Stress Resilience

Develop tools to navigate life's challenges. Stress management is key to lasting health.

Stay Strong
  • Learn and practice breathing techniques
  • Create healthy stress outlets
  • Set boundaries to protect your energy
Design Your Environment

Shape your surroundings to support health. Your environment influences your choices.

Create Space
  • Make healthy choices the easy choices
  • Create spaces for movement and rest
  • Minimize exposure to toxins
Nurture Social Health

Cultivate supportive relationships. Social connections are vital for longevity.

Connect More
  • Build a health-supporting community
  • Share healthy activities with others
  • Practice active listening and empathy
Practice Preventive Care

Take proactive steps for long-term health. Prevention is better than cure.

Stay Ahead
  • Schedule regular health check-ups
  • Stay current with preventive screenings
  • Learn your family health history
Embrace Natural Rhythms

Align with your body's natural cycles. Working with nature supports optimal health.

Flow Better
  • Sync activities with your energy levels
  • Get natural light exposure daily
  • Adjust habits with seasons
Celebrate Progress

Acknowledge your health journey with gratitude. Every step forward counts.

Celebrate Now
  • Track and celebrate small victories
  • Practice body gratitude daily
  • Share successes with supporters

True health wealth isn't measured by absence of illness, but by the vitality, resilience, and joy we experience in body, mind, and spirit. When we invest in our health, we invest in our capacity to live life fully.
